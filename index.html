Our Donate Plan

We have different categories of donation plans. You can contribute to our donation plan. Every different category has different types of work. Our donate plans are these. To know more about please check it out.

Food for family

In this plan anybody can contribute to help disaster people by giving us money or food for them. You can send us any kind of dry food, fruits, nuts and pure drinking water etc.

Shelter for a family

In this plan anybody can contribute to help disaster people for shelter by giving us money or also giving us home construction materials and goods.

Education for a child

In this plan anybody can contribute to help disaster child education by giving us money or education accessories. This plan is only for child education.
